About Me

Hi! I'm a passionate tech enthusiast and avid gamer with over 8 years of experience in the technology industry. I've worked as a software developer, tested countless gaming peripherals, and built multiple gaming rigs from scratch.

My journey started with modding my first PC at age 16, and since then, I've been obsessed with finding the perfect balance between performance and value. Whether it's discovering budget-friendly gaming accessories that punch above their weight class or diving deep into the latest tech trends, I'm here to share honest, practical insights.

When I'm not coding or writing reviews, you'll find me exploring indie games, competing in online tournaments, or tinkering with the latest gadgets. My mission is simple: help fellow tech and gaming enthusiasts make informed decisions without breaking the bank.
