Summary: G502 HERO is a fantastic ergonomic mouse with a top‑tier sensor and deep customizability, but it’s much heavier than budget picks. If you value lightweight flicks for FPS, stick to G203/G305; if you want extra buttons and comfort for long sessions or MMO/MOBA, the G502 HERO is worth the bump in price.

Notes on Price & Availability

All models above routinely sell for under $50 as of August 22, 2025. Pricing changes by color/edition and retailer promos. If a specific color sits over $50, check another color or wait for a sale.
